The Governing Board

The governing board of the University is vested in the Board of Regents composed of the following

  1. Chairman of the Commission on Higher Education (CHED) as Chairman
  2. President of the University as Vice Chairman
  3. Chairman of the Senate Committee on Education, Culture and Arts
  4. Chairman of the House Committee on Higher and Technical Education
  5. Chairman of the House Committee on Higher and Technical Education (NEDA)
  6. Regional Director of the Department of Agriculture
  7. Regional Director of Science and Technology
  8. President of the Federated Faculty Association
  9. President of the Federated Alumni Association
  10. President of the PSU-Federated Student Government (PSU-FSG)
  11. Two (2) prominent citizens in the community

The Administrative Council

The Administrative Council is composed of the President of the University as Chairman, the Vice Presidents, Campus Executive Directors University Directors, and other officials of equal rank as members and whose duty is to review and recommend to the Board of Regents policies governing the administration, management and development plans of the University for appropriate action.

The University Academic Council

The University Academic Council is composed of the President of the University as Chairman and the members of the instruction with the rank of not lower than Assistant Professor.

The University Academic Council shall have the power to review and recommend the curricular offerings and rules of discipline of the University subject to appropriate action of the Board of Regents. It shall fix the requirements for the admission of students as well as for graduation and the conferment of degrees subject to review and/ or approval of the Board through the President of the University.

Administration

The administration of the University is vested in the President who is assisted by the four Vice Presidents namely: the Vice President for Academic Affairs (VPAA), Vice President for Administration and Planning (VPAP), Vice resident for Financial Management (VPFM) and Vice President for Research and Extension (VPRE)

The VPAP is in charge of the following divisions namely: Administrative Services, Engineering, University Supply Services, Physical Plant and Facilities, Human Resource Management, Auxiliary and Public Relation, Publication and Information Office. The VPAA oversees the effective implementation of policies, programs and projects related to instruction and research in the University as well as the Student Services, Admission, Guidance and Testing, Sports Development On the other hand, Training Institute, Local and International Linkages, Alumni Affairs, Community Extension, Medical and Dental Services, Culture, Arts and Pangasinan Heritage Studies, Registrar and Learning Resource Center are the divisions directly under the VPRE. The VPFM is directly in-charge of the following isions: Finance Division, Production, Marketing and Resource Generation Budget, Accounting, Procurement and University Cashier

The different campuses are managed by the Executive Directors with the support of the College Deans. The policy making body for academic matters in each campus rests in its Campus Academic Council whose policies and decisions are forwarded for approval of the Board of Regents through the University Academic Council. Plans and decisions are related to the Administrative Council composed of the University President, Vice Presidents, Campus Executive Directors, University Directors, the University Board Secretary, and the Chief Administrative Officer.
