Student Code of Discipline

Section 1. The code shall apply to all members of the student body for offenses committed within and outside the premises of the University

Section 2. Discipline. Discipline is inherent in human nature. The internal type of discipline which is not imposed by sanctions but is dictated by the individuals own sense of right and wrong is the best motivation to desirable conduct and behavior

Section 3. Disciplinary measures are meant to effect better interpersonal relationships in the academic community. However, for the purpose of having a common guide that shall govem the acts of students of the PSU and reflect the true image of a Filipino, this code of discipline has been prepared. General Provisions

Section 4. A student shall be reprimanded, suspended or expelled for lawful cause after due process of law

Section 5. All matters relating to the conduct and discipline of students shall be subject to the jurisdiction of the office of the Guidance Counselor, Coordinator of Student Services, the Campus Executive Director, and the Director of Student Services and Alumni Affairs and the University President in that order

Section 6. The Student Disciplinary Tribunal (college level) shall conduct formal investigation of student cases referred to by the Coordinator of Student Services and other offices. This Tribunal shall be composed of the Guidance Counselor the Associate Dean of the faculty representative and a student representative. Their findings and recommendations shall be forwarded to the Campus Executive Director, University Director for Student Services and Alumni Affairs and the University President, for disciplinary action.

Section 7. The decision on student disciplinary cases shall reside in the University President upon recommendation of the Student Disciplinary Tribunal.
