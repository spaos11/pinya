Student Code of Conduct

Section 1. Discipline is a means to guide and liberate students to positively channel their mind, heart and behavior.

Section 2. To Achieve and maintain peace and order, decency and academic excellence, and good moral character, each student shall follow the rules and regulations; whether on campus or outside. Any deviation from the expected norms of student behavior is dealt with proper disciplinary action.

Conduct Outside the University

Section 3. PSU students project the highly established image of the University, uphold and defend its integrity and excellence.

Section 4. Students shall voluntarily participate actively in socio-civic programs and activities, symposia, conferences, for a, conventions and religious activities in the community.

Section 5. Students are encouraged to perform their civic duties by giving sound opinions about public issues and concerns and helping with their local officials in the implementation of such policies that are directed towards the improvement of their community.

Section 6. Students demonstrate their charitable and brotherly spirit by helping their colleagues in their distress.

Section 7. Permission shall be secured from the University President upon favorable recommendation of the Director for the Student Services and Alumni Affairs prior to the undertaking any activity outside the University sponsored by any University recognized organization. Students attending such activities shall present a written permit or waiver duly signed by parents or guardians. Participating students on such occasions shall always observe proper decorum.

Conduct Inside the University

Section 8. PSU students respect authority and all those who vested in it.

Section 9. The faculty of the University are persons in authority, an authority that as been vested upon them by law. Cognizant to such authority, students give their full cooperation and respect to their instructors/professors in pursuance to respect the University goals.

Section 10. Students always wear their identification card (ID) and proper uniform days) upon entry of the University premises and shall be worn at all times in the University campus.

Section 11. Students strive to be neat, clean and well groomed at all times wearing modest, simple and decent attire.

Section 12. Students observe silence in the library, conference halls, corridors and study areas and avoid unnecessary noise, boisterous laughter and conversations, whistling and the like.

Section 13. Students refrain from the use of vulgar, indecent and offensive words, for utterance of such words are unbecoming of respectable men and women.

Section 14. Students are encouraged to help keep the University and its premises clean and tidy and avoid untidy habits and practices like spitting, scattering of pieces and cigarette butts, littering. marking of walls/furniture and sitting on railings.

Section 15. Students are encouraged to handle and use all properties of the University with utmost care for these are assets to provide student comfort and to support their academic, physical, civic and spiritual growth and development.

Section 16. Students know the Philippine National Anthem, the Pangasinan Hymn and the PSU Hymn by heart, they recite and/or sing these hymns with the fervor and sincerity.

Section 17. Students shall at no time bring any alcoholic beverages nor any prohibited drugs into the premises of the University.

Section 18. Students shall not enter the University premises under the influence of any intoxicated drink or any prohibited drug Conduct in the Classroom

Section 19. Students shall report to the classroom on the scheduled time and room. The class is considered dismissed when the instructor/professor is not in the classroom within the first fifteen minutes of the class period.

Section 20. Students shall refrain from smoking and eating messy and crunchy food inside the classroom and laboratories.

Section 21. Only students duly enrolled in a subject are admitted in the classroom.

Section 22. Respect, courtesy, prudence and politeness to instructor/professor fellow students shall always be observed at all times in the classroom.

Section 23. Students shall willfully participate in intellectual discussions with their instructors/professors and peers with sincerity, clarity, frankness and friendliness.

Section 24. Students manifest self-respect, personal integrity and observe honor system during examinations. They conduct themselves in a manner that their honesty is beyond suspicion.
