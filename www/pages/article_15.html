Grievance Procedures

Section 1. A grievance reported orally shall be considered informal and shall be settled informally. Only written, duly signed, and notarized complaints formal which require a formal procedure for settlement. become

Section 2. All proceedings (complaints, answers, findings, recommendations decisions, and excerpts of proceedings) shall be in writing and signed by al parties concerned. All records shall be in the possession of the Vice President for Academic Affairs.

Section 3. The following procedures should be followed in cases of complaint:

A. Individual complaint against a faculty member

1. The student consults the concerned faculty member and explains his written complaint.
2. If the student feels he has not obtained the necessary action, he sees the department chairperson
3. If still he has not obtained the necessary action, he sees the Campus Executive Director who must investigate the matter objectively from both sides.
b.Class complaint against a faculty member

1. At least majority (50% plus one) of the class submits a duly signed formal complaint to the department chairperson of the campus. A committee composed of three (3) chosen by the class must see the department chairperson who has given 48 hours to investigate and solve the complaint.
2. If the students feel that their complaints has not been acted upon after the prescribed time, the class representatives shall present their written complaint to the Campus Executive Director who has given 48 hours to investigate and solve the complaint.
3. If the students still feel that no action has been done after tha prescribed time, the class representatives shall present their written complaint to the Vice-President for Academic and Research who wi designate, in 24 hours, a Student Grievance Committee composed of the following:
A. Official representative of Campus Faculty Association
b. Two student representatives;
c. A senior member of the concened department, and
d. A third party mutually acceptable to the students and the respondent faculty member
4. The Committee must render its decision after 72 hours of investigation and submit its recommendations to the Vice President for Academic Affairs from which he will base the decision
C. A student's complaint against a Campus Executive Director
1. The student forwards the written complaint to the Campus Executive Director concerned who must acknowledge receipt of the complaint in writing, The Campus Executive Director has 72 hours to settle complaint.
2. If the student feels that action has not been done after the prescribed time, he shall present his grievance to the Vice President for Academic Affairs who has 48 hours to investigate and settle the complaint.
